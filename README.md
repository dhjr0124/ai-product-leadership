# AI Product Leadership

Operational artifacts for leading and scaling AI product organizations. 
The documents in this repository are the things I deploy when standing up 
or inheriting an AI product team — not strategy abstractions, but the 
concrete plans, rubrics, and rituals that make the work happen.
